Audience

Persona 1

Picture

Demographics

"Sandra" is a 52-year-old woman who has recently started her own business.

Motivation

The fifty-two (52) young and ambitious entrepreneurs, 52% of whom are women, will receive grants from US$ 500 up to US$3,000 each to either grow their business, launch a new business, or pursue innovations that allow them to scale up their businesses in order to attract customers and fully commercialize their products.

Persona 2

Picture

Demographics

"Steve" is a 40-year-old investor.

Motivation

Steve is motivated by a desire to succeed.

Steve wants to find stable, local companies who want to grow so she can invest in them. She knows the area is full of good opportunities, but she doesn't know where to strat looking.

Steve wants to look at the list of businesses who have already joined the chamber of commerce so she can do some research. She also wants to engage with the board of directors about possibly advertising her services.

Steve needs to find local companies and engage with local leadership of the chamber.
